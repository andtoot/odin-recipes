Vegan Shepherds Pie

Description

Here we have a tried and true vegan shepherd's pie recipe sure to be a hit at any gathering or even just a great dish to have at home. We will be using a meat substitute called Beyond Meat that adds a great texture and flavor. Unlike your typical meat pie there is no crust, but instead a nice layer of luffy mashed potatoes on top. It is recomended to use our mashed potatoes recipe also on this site for a great vegan combo!

Ingredients

Listed here will be all the ingredients needed for everythin except the mashed potatoes.

  • 1 pack of beyond meat
  • 1 pack of beyond sausage
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 pack of whatever vegan cheese you prefer
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 4 large carrots
  • 1 pack of frozen peas
  • 1 pack of fresh white or brown mushrooms
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • worcestershire sauce(vegan)
  • black pepper
  • salt

Instructions

  1. pour 2 tbl spn of olive oil int a large pan and begin heating it on medium heat Begin chopping the onion. You can chop them to whatever size you like I personally prefer to make them smaller. You should also take this time to chop the garlic, carrots, and beyond sausage.
  2. When the oil is hot begin carmelizing onions and carrots. Once the onions have begun to turn a golden brown you can add the frozen peas, garlic, beyond sausage, beyond ground meat, mushrooms, salt, and pepper.
  3. Once everything is fully cooked add the can of tomato paste and stir it into everything. Add 1 cup of water and sautee for 30 minutes. Add flour if it needs to be thicker.
  4. Make mashed potatoes and grate cheese if needed. Get a large deep baking dish and pour all of the contents from the pan into the dish. Evenly spread the mashed potatoes on top. Sprikle the chese over the top of everything.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for 2 hours
